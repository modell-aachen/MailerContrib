

---++ MailerContribPlugin

This is a 'mini' plugin that makes the MailerContrib =mailnotify= bin-script into a =rest= handler

It can be called from the command line as:
   * (in the =foswiki/bin= dir) =./rest MailerContribPlugin.notify=
   
or from a browser (presuming sufficient privilege):
   * %SCRIPTURL{rest}%/MailerContribPlugin/notify
